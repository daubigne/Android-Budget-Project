Student Budget App v0.3 Release Notes
========================
Released on Oct 22, 2012

Requirements
--------------------
Smartphone with Android 2.2 or higher

Added features
--------------------
* Options menu
* Edit your account balance
* Remove transactions
* Reset transactions and budget items

Known bugs and limtations
--------------------
* Removing categories hides all budget-items and transactions from the lists and opening graphs makes the app crash* Duplicate budget-items when changing category
* Budget value disappears when adding while editing
* Adding future transaction makes graph work incorrectly
