Student Budget App v0.2 Release Notes
========================
Released on Oct 15, 2012

Requirements
--------------------
Smartphone with Android 1.4 or higher

Added features
--------------------
* A home screen displaying the amount of money you've got left to spend.
* Add a transaction.
* List transactions by income and expenses.
* View changes of your account balance in line graphs.

Known bugs and limtations
--------------------
* The minimum target api level 11 because of the use of an action bar.
* Cannot choose category for transactions and budget items.
* There is not content in the options tab.
* Only 10 months of history are available in graphs.
* Graphs are not displaying correctly if there are no transactions one month.
