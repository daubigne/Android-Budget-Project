Student Budget App v0.1 Release Notes
========================
Released on Oct 8, 2012

Requirements
--------------------
Smartphone with Android 1.4 or higher

Added features
--------------------
* Manage your budget by entering predicted income and expenses.
* Divide your budget in predefined subcategories.
* Add transactions to keep track of your real income and expenses.

Known limitations
--------------------
* Adding transactions do not work.
* Can not choose category for budget items.
* Only two of five tabs have interactive content.
